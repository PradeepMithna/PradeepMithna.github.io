Privacy Policy

Effective July 15, 2026

MARK is a clean, utility-focused attendance and schedule tracker built for college students. This policy outlines exactly how your data is processed, keeping clarity and transparency at the core.
01 Account Creation

MARK offers an optional sign-in service to securely back up your data. While you can use the core app completely offline without entering personal details, logging in helps safeguard your records.

ℹ No mandatory profiles, no contact lists, or background social access required.
02 Local Attendance Processing

Your subject logs, daily percentages, and modified slots stay securely inside your device architecture. All calculations are executed locally to protect your data privacy.

03 Data Storage & Backup

MARK stores configurations like theme customizations, pre-existing initial logs, and subject arrays directly on local storage. When signed in, safe cloud infrastructure synchronizes these settings securely.

04 Children's Privacy

MARK is intended strictly as a general academic productivity utility. We do not knowingly target or harvest personal details from users under the age of 13.

05 Data Retention

Your attendance data stays intact on your device until the app is deleted or reset. Secure cloud-backed architecture honors direct user commands to purge tracking history instantly.
